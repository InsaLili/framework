Step3/4: add indicators to the activity

Instruction: in this step, you can add indicators to the application. The indicators would be provided for students and teachers.

  • Indicators for students: you can choose the indicators to help students' regulate their action and raise their awareness of the on-going activity. For example, in the restricted sequence, you can add the progress bar to help students better be aware of the progress. You can also add gamification elements to motivate students and gain entertainment to the activity. For example, a timer badge to encourage students to finish tasks within the limited time; or a "comment badge" to encourage students to submit more comments.
  • Indicators for teachers: in the activity, you will have a page to monitor your students behaviors. You can choose which indicators you want to monitor students behaviors. For example, to see the number of checking locations of each students, and the comments their add.
