In this step, you can add indicators and gamification elements to the application. For example:

  • Indicators: indicators would be used to monitor students' action, meanwhile encourage students to regulate their behaviors. For example, you can add progress bar to the activity to help students be aware of the progress.
  • Gaminifaction elements: gamification elements are used to motivate students and gain entertainment to the task. For example, a timer badge to encourage students to finish tasks within limited time; or a "comment badge" to stimulate students to submit more comments.
