Step2/3: define sequence and mechanism of the activity

A decision making process awlays contains following tasks.

  • T0 (optional): define criteria for evaluating alternatives. It is a prerequisite for a desicion making process. Besides the criteria you defined for students (in the former step) and you can let student add criteria by themselves.
  • T1: analyze and evaluate each alternative;
  • T2: compare alternatives and make decision;
  • T3 (optional): evaluate results.

In this part, you will define how students are going to carry on these tasks to finish the activity.

Seq1: Restricted sequence

  • T0 (optional): define criteria for evaluating alternatives;
  • T1: analyze and evaluate each alternative;
  • T2: compare alternatives and make decision;
  • T3: evaluate results.

(Students have to evaluate all the alternatives in T1 before they can move to T2 to choose the alternative.)

Seq2: Unestricted sequence

  • T0 (optional): define criteria for evaluating alternatives;
  • T1 & T2: analyze, evaluate alternatives and make decision;
  • T2: evaluate results.

(T1 and T2 are mixed, students can make the choice while they are checking and evaluating alternatives. In this sequence, each alternative only needs to be evaluated once.)
